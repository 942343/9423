Five Nights at Freddy's

Fullscreen

Description:

Five Nights at Freddy's is a chilling survival horror game where you’re locked inside a spooky pizza restaurant with animatronic creatures that come to life after dark. As a nighttime security guard, you must use surveillance cameras and limited resources to survive five nights. With its minimalist gameplay, eerie sound design, and constant tension, FNAF has become a fan-favorite among horror game lovers.
